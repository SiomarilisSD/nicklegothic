History of the font

Based on lettering found on the 1918 same chinese banknote its serifed counterpart Nickel. It is also based of lettering from midcentury sans serif like Microgramma and Eurostile, and 70s gothic squared font, Neographik nad Serpentine. It's a display sans serif. Containing Blocky shapes, it communicates with raw power.
